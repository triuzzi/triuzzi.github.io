Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tab Shortcuts does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. The extension operates entirely locally in your browser. No analytics, no tracking, no network requests.

Permissions

  • tabs — Duplicate and pin tabs
  • activeTab — Interact with the current tab when you trigger a command
  • commands — Register keyboard shortcuts
  • scripting — Inject the prevent-close functionality into the active tab on demand
  • host_permissions (github.com) — Collapse translation files and convert timestamps on GitHub pages

For questions or concerns, open an issue on GitHub.
